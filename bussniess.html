Business.

What is Business?

An activity where you sell goods or services to make money.

Key Parts

• Idea → What you offer • Customers → Who buys it • Value → Why they choose you • Revenue → Money earned • Costs → Money spent • Profit → Revenue − Costs

Types of Business

• Sole Proprietorship → One owner • Partnership → Two or more owners • Company/Corporation → Separate legal entity • Online Business → Run mainly on the internet
