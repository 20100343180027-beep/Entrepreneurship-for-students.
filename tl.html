What is entrepreneurship?

Entrepreneurship is the process of creating, developing, and running a new business in order to make a profit, solve problems, or bring new ideas to life.

Key Points Innovation: Coming up with new ideas or improving existing products/services. Risk-taking: Entrepreneurs take financial or personal risks to start a business. Management: Planning, organizing, and running the business efficiently. Opportunity recognition: Spotting problems or gaps in the market and finding solutions.

“Chase the vision, not the money; the money will end up following you.” – Tony Hsieh (Founder of Zappos).Focus on passion and creating something meaningful; success and profit will naturally follow.
